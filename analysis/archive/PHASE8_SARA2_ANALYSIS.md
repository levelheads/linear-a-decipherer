## Executive Summary

This analysis investigates SA-RA2 (20 occurrences, 100% at Hagia Triada) and the broader S-R paradigm to understand:
1. What contexts SA-RA2 appears in
2. Functional relationships within the S-R paradigm
3. Whether functional equivalents exist at other sites (KH, ZA, PH)

**Key Findings**:
- SA-RA2 is an **allocation marker** appearing before COMMODITY + NUMERAL (18/20 = 90%)
- The S-R paradigm contains **9 members** but they show **functional divergence**, not coherent paradigm
- SA-RA2 is **100% HT-exclusive** but S-R variants appear at 4 sites (HT, PKZ, ZA, SAMW)
- **No direct functional equivalent** found at KH for SA-RA2's allocation function
- A-DU appears at both HT and KH in pre-logogram position - potential candidate for broader distribution

**Conclusion**: S-R paradigm shows **formal** relationship (shared consonants) but NOT **functional** paradigm like K-R.

- **SI-RU**: Personal name
- **SA-RI**: Personal name OR variant

## 7. Conclusions

### 7.1 SA-RA2 Function: CONFIRMED

**SA-RA2 = Allocation marker** (PROBABLE confidence)

Evidence:
- 90% pre-logogram context (before COMMODITY + NUMERAL)
- Multi-commodity distribution (GRA, CYP, OLE, VIR)
- Follows KU-RO (totals) and personal names
- Structural parallel to Akkadian palace economy terminology

### 7.2 Geographic Distribution: HT-SPECIFIC

| Term | HT | Non-HT |
|------|----|----|
| SA-RA2 | 20 (100%) | 0 (0%) |
| All S-R | 34 (89%) | 4 (11%) |

SA-RA2 is an **HT-specific administrative term**. The broader S-R paradigm has limited spread to PKZ, ZA, SAMW.

### 7.3 S-R Paradigm: NOT COHERENT

Unlike K-R (KU-RO/KI-RO with clear total/deficit contrast), the S-R forms do NOT show:
- Consistent vowel-function mapping
- Shared semantic domain
- Paradigmatic alternation

**Recommendation**: Treat SA-RA2 as distinct lexeme, not paradigm member.

### 7.4 Functional Equivalents: NOT FOUND

No term at KH, ZA, or PH performs the same function as SA-RA2.

**Implications**:
1. Other sites may have had different allocation terminology
2. Or allocation was marked differently (contextually, not lexically)
3. Document type bias may affect comparability

### 7.5 Universal vs. HT-Specific: HT-SPECIFIC

**SA-RA2 is NOT a universal Linear A administrative term**.

It appears to be specific to Hagia Triada's scribal/accounting practice, possibly reflecting:
- HT's role as redistribution center
- Specialized accounting vocabulary
- Possible Akkadian loanword (regional adoption)
