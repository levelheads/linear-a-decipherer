## 8. Key Discoveries

### 8.1 Demeter and Athena Worship Origins

The presence of DA-MA-TE (Demeter) and A-TA-NA (Athena) in Linear A confirms:

1. **Minoan Origins**: These goddess names predate Greek Linear B by 200+ years
2. **Continuity**: Greek religion inherited Minoan deity names
3. **Pre-Greek Substrate**: Demeter/Athena are likely Pre-Greek names adopted by Greeks

### 8.2 Naming System Features

Linear A personal names show:
1. **Suffix-based gender marking** (-NA/-NE feminine)
2. **Theophoric compounding** (deity + suffix)
3. **Multi-lingual elements** (Semitic + Pre-Greek + Luwian)
4. **Occupational/title function** (many may not be personal names)
