## Key Patterns Identified

### 1. HTW Sealing Group

I-RA2, SI-KA, and DA-KA all appear exclusively in the HTWa (Hagia Triada West) sealing series:
- All LMIB period
- Likely commodity labels or administrative designations
- Function similar to modern shipping labels

### 2. -RA2 Ending Pattern

Words ending in -RA2: SA-RA2, I-RA2, PU-RA2
- May constitute a morphological class
- SA-RA2 = "allocation" (previously established)
- Suggests I-RA2 and PU-RA2 may be related transaction types

### 3. A-initial Pattern

Many short A-initial words show strong Semitic scores:
- A-NE, A-RA, A-MI, A-MA, A-RI
- The A- prefix may indicate:
  - Semitic definite article (ha-/al-)
  - Luwian coordinative conjunction
  - Pre-Greek prothetic vowel
