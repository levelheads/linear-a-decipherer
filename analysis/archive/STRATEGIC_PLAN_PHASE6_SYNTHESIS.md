## 2. Synthesis of Phases 1-5

### 2.1 Phase 1: Paradigm Completion

**S-R Paradigm** (51 occurrences):
- SA-RA₂ is functionally distinct (allocation marker)
- SA-RU, SA-RO, SA-RI do NOT form coherent paradigm
- SI-RU-TE is religious vocabulary

**D-R Paradigm** (29 occurrences):
- DA-RE promoted to POSSIBLE (cross-site, 100% medial)
- DA-RI-DA shows reduplication (grammatical process)

**K-P Paradigm** (60+ occurrences):
- Does NOT replicate K-R semantic polarity
- KU-PA₃-NU at PROBABLE (personnel term)
- KA-PA shows header position pattern

**Key Finding**: U/I total/deficit polarity is **K-R specific**, not universal.

### 2.2 Phase 2: Promotion Campaign

**Promoted**:

- SU-PU at PROBABLE (6 occ, cross-site)
- Theophoric names at HIGH (Linear B cognates)

**Demoted**:
- **KA-RO-PA₃**: PROBABLE → **POSSIBLE** (hapax)

### 2.3 Phase 3: Religious Register

**Canonical Libation Formula** (IOZa2 exemplar):
```
1. A-TA-I-*301-WA-JA (invocation)
2. JA-DI-KI-TU (secondary)
3. JA-SA-SA-RA-ME (divine name)
4. U-NA-KA-NA-SI (epithet)
5. I-PI-NA-MA + SI-RU-TE (ritual closing)
```

**Key Findings**:
- 96 unique religious vocabulary terms
- 59 inscriptions from peak sanctuaries
- Religious register has NO numerals
- Pre-Greek substrate dominant in divine names

### 2.4 Phase 4: Personal Names

**Database Statistics**:

**Theophoric Confirmation**:
- DA-MA-TE = Demeter (Minoan origin)
- A-TA-NA = Athena (Minoan origin)
- Both predate Greek by 200+ years
